Gita Jayanti

We commemorate Gita Jayanti on Mokshada Ekadasi because it was on this day, around 5000 years ago, that Lord Krishna gave his great follower Arjuna the most important and secret knowledge of the Bhagavad Gita for devotional service during the war of Kurukshetra. He spoke to him in an effort to increase awareness of life and its meaning. He also explained to him how to give up in the proper manner. Every year in December, people celebrate it.

During this month, devotees serve the Lord by distributing Bhagwad Gita to the residents of Dwarka and nearby areas. Lord Krishna, in Bhagwad Gita, says that whosoever spreads My message to the world, is the most dear person to Me. So, devotees selflessly engage in Bhagwad Gita distribution, to get lord Krishna’s mercy.
